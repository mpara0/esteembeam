More Help

If you think you are struggling with something more serious, we recommend you get more help. Here are some resources that can help you figure out what to do.

If you need someone to talk to, here is a help line that you call: 1-800-273-8255

To find out about steps you can take if you have a serious problem, visit these helpful websites:

  • Making yourself aware
  • Finding Help
  • Immediate Help
  • How to get help

None of these websites are ours, they belong to outside sources such as Mayo Clinic, Mental Health America, and U.S. Department of Health and Human Services.
